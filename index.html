The working of timetec cloud ecosystem

Click Here

Company A is using TimeTec TA and TimeTec Leave to manage its staff force of around 2000. The Company also deployed TimeTec Hire for staff recruitment with all the employees information centralized at TimeTec Profile.

Biometrics hardware (FingerTec) are physically installed at all entrances to ease attendance clockings. TimeTec Access and TimeTec VMS are connected to the same hardware to manage access control for both employees and visitors. The Company also using TimeTec QF Plus & TimeTec BLE-2 or BLE-16 for doors, turnstiles and elevators to provide more user credential options such as QR code and face recognition.

The guards at the HQ are also using TimeTec Patrol to perform real time guard tour routines. When a pre-registered visitor entered the HQ’s parking site, his TimeTec Parking App will prompt TimeTec i-Ad and i-Merchants promotions, and a notification will send to his host to announce his arrival.

The same visitor if decided to visit his friend whom is staying at the nearby neighbourhood that is using i-Neighbour (integrated with i-Account), he can use the same App to pre-register himself. EpiCamera as a cloud surveillance system, i-TimeTec as a smart home/office IoT platform can be integrated with TimeTec Patrol, TimeTec Access, and i-Neighbour to improve overall security and automation.
